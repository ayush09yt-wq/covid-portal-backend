covid 19 and history

Global Origin

Where: Wuhan, Hubei, China

When: December 2019

Details: Initial cases linked to a seafood and live animal market. Identified as a new coronavirus (SARS-CoV-2).

Jan 30, 2020: WHO declared Public Health Emergency of International Concern
Mar 11, 2020: WHO declared global pandemic

COVID-19 in India

First Case: 30 January 2020, Kerala (student returned from Wuhan)

Early Spread: Few isolated cases in February; multiple states reported cases in March 2020

22 March 2020: Janata Curfew (1-day trial lockdown)
25 March 2020: Nationwide lockdown announced
